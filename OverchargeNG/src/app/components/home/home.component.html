Welcome to Revature Overcharge!

Revature Overcharge is a study system for Revature employees. Users can create their own flashcard decks and use decks created by other users to study. When running through a deck, a user can type their answer before viewing the answer to a flashcard, giving them the ability to compare their answer with the real one. Flashcards can be marked as mastered to prevent requestioning. Decks can be rated. A timer is also provided to coordinate study and break sessions. Daily and weekly objectives are measured to award points to users.
